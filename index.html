About Me:

Hi, I'm Katie! I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area. After attending several colleges around the area, I relocated to Southern California to study Finance at California State University at Fullerton. I worked for several years managing the financial books and offices of several design and construction businesses.

After relocating to Austin, Texas with my husband Tim, I shifted my focus toward our growing a family. We now have two beautiful children as well as a collection of pets (including chickens!). I stayed busy working part-time contract bookkeeping until deciding to switch fields in 2018.

I am currently learning full stack web development at The UT Austin Coding Bootcamp. I believe my natural love of creativity, knack for building and fixing, and attention to small details makes for a perfect fit in the ever-growing world of software development.
