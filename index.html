Hi there, I'm Katie. I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, in a small town named Gilroy - garlic capitol of the world! After high school and community college, I spent some time at California State University Fullerton studying finance. I worked for several years managing the financial books and offices of several design and construction businesses.

In 2008 I relocated to Austin, Texas with my husband Tim and shifted my focus toward our growing family. We now have two beautiful children as well as a collection of pets, including several chickens. I stayed busy working part-time contract bookkeeping until deciding to switch fields in 2018.

I am currently learning front- and back-end web development at The UT Austin Coding Bootcamp. I believe my natural love of creativity, knack for building and fixing, and attention to small details makes for a perfect fit in the ever-growing world of software development.
